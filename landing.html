Our story

Built in memory.
Shared with love.

This platform was created after the passing of my mother and my wife — two of the most beloved people in my life. When they passed, I wanted to do something meaningful. I wanted to organise the community, bring people together, and make it easy for everyone to contribute their recitations and duas in their memory.

But organising it was hard. People didn't know which Juz had been claimed. Recitations were being done but never tracked. There was no central place for the family to see the love pouring in. So I built one.

"In loving memory of my mother and my wife — offered as Sadaqah Jariyah. May Allah grant them the highest place in Jannah, and may every recitation made through this platform reach them. Ameen." — The founder

What started as something personal has grown into a platform for all Muslim families and mosques. Because every family deserves a beautiful, dignified way to honour their loved ones — and every recitation deserves to be counted.
